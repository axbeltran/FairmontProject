Monthly Meetings Main Page

Welcome to the Monthly Meetings Page! Here you can watch and learn about Fairmont's latest developments, upcoming events, and other news that may be trending around the community.

If you look on the left, you will see a column for for each year, with a dropdown for each corresponding month. Click on that month to get access to that month's meeting. Each page will contain a meeting summary, breaking down what was discussed in that meeting, as well as any upcoming events discussed.

Also included is a video of the meeting itself, so if you prefer to watch rather than listen, then that option is available for you.
